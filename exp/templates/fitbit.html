Fitbit Data Access

Before continuing, please sync your Fitbit data now via the Fitbit app on your mobile device. You may need to turn Bluetooth on in order to sync your data. This will provide us with the most up-to-date snapshot of your fitness profile!

Once you have synced your device, please click the button below to provide us with temporary read-only access to your Fitbit data. You will need to sign in using your Fitbit username and password and click the “select all” box in order to proceed. For more information about what information we will collect from you and how we will use it, please view our Privacy Policy. (If you do not wish to provide access to your Fitbit data, you must discontinue your participation in this HIT.) We agree not to collect personally identifiable information from your Fitbit account, and your data will be used only for research purposes (e.g. to understand how your health and fitness history relates to your memory performance on various tasks and games).
